Meal ideas
Nutrition advice
You already eat well ✨
Just make protein intentional at every meal. You're most of the way there already.
Gluten free is handled
All meal ideas use rice, rice noodles, kumara or GF alternatives. No wheat, no problem.
Chicken is your best friend
150g cooked chicken breast = ~45g protein. Two serves a day and you're basically there. Add eggs and Greek yoghurt for the rest.
Pre-training
Banana or GF rice cakes + nut butter 45 min before. Light and easy to digest.
Post-training
Protein within 45 min — chicken, Greek yoghurt, protein shake, or eggs.
Hydration
2-3L water daily. Electrolytes after stair master sessions. Skin loves hydration.
Don't overthink it
Protein at breakfast, lunch and dinner and the rest takes care of itself. No calorie counting needed.
Training phases
Stair master guide 🏆
Beginner (Weeks 1-4)
20-25 min at level 6-8. Focus on posture — don't lean on the rails.
Building (Weeks 5-10)
30 min at level 8-10. Add 1 min intervals at level 12 every 5 min.
Progressed (Weeks 11+)
35-40 min at level 10-12. Try 2 min on / 1 min off intervals.
Form tip
Stand tall, core on, light touch on rails only for balance. Drive through the heel of each step for maximum glute activation.
